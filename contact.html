Who We Are

GreenGo is an informative search platform for bike lanes in Brisbane, offering cycling lovers with up-to-date bike laneinformation while fostering a shared passion for cycling, inspiring users to discover new routes and enhance their bikingexperience.

GreenGo Team

Our team was well-rounded, with a diverse mix of skills and backgrounds, all sharing a passion for exploring the city. This fueled our creativity and led to the development of GreenGo, making the biking experience for city exploration easier and more engaging for users.
